For C++, a library called C-Turtle is used to provide an equivalent of Python's turtle. It acts as a close replacement to provide easy to use graphics to C++, while maintaining the objects and commands you might be used to in Python. It was developed by Jesse Walker-Schadler at Berea College during the summer of 2019, and can be found on GitHub here: https://github.com/walkerje/C-Turtle 1
